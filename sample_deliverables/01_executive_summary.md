# Executive Summary

## Customer Overview
- Customer: Northstar Manufacturing
- Industry: Manufacturing
- Market: Global / multi-site operations
- Audience: Executive stakeholders, internal communications leaders, IT evaluators
- Demo objective: Show how Staffbase could be branded to support frontline communication, leadership messaging, and operational updates

## Brand Interpretation
- Brand tone: Dependable, modern, operationally strong
- Visual direction: Clean enterprise layout with confident blue leadership, dark industrial structure, and controlled teal accents
- Primary visual cues: Strong branded navigation, structured content cards, practical mobile-first communication surfaces
- Confidence level of palette: Sample / predefined for demo purposes

## Design Strategy
- The visual system uses a strong blue as the main brand expression, supported by a dark navy for structure and a teal accent for action and emphasis
- The layout is designed to feel realistic for manufacturing communications: plant updates, safety notices, leadership messages, and shift information
- The UI is intentionally polished but restrained so it can be used in a commercial presentation without feeling overdesigned

## Staffbase Fit
- Standard branding can represent most of the visual identity: main branding color, navigation header colors, accent color, and menu-related areas
- Custom CSS is reserved for refinement areas such as bottom navigation emphasis, badge styling, and selected component polish
- The mock includes demo-only visual treatment that is intentionally separated from Staffbase-compatible CSS suggestions

## Assumptions
- The customer palette is treated as a demo brand system, not a verified public brand guide
- The sign-in screen is optional and not included in the core flow
- The mobile experience is considered important due to likely frontline and distributed workforce usage

## Recommended Next Step
- Validate the preferred brand tone with the account team
- Replace placeholder content with customer-specific communication examples
- Use this package as the first-pass demo baseline for future manufacturing prospects
