## Suggested demo flow
1. Open the branded home/news experience
2. Show how navigation reflects the customer identity
3. Highlight mobile experience and bottom navigation
4. Show an article or page detail view
5. Explain what is immediate branding vs what is CSS refinement

## Suggested talk track
- “This concept is designed to feel recognizably yours from the first screen.”
- “We started by mapping your brand into standard Staffbase branding areas.”
- “Where the standard settings are not enough, we identified a small and controlled Custom CSS layer.”
- “This lets us balance realism, speed, and brand fit in a practical rollout discussion.”

## Likely customer reactions to prepare for
- “Can this be done without heavy development?”
- “How much of this is standard configuration?”
- “Would mobile look consistent?”
- “Can the sign-in experience also be branded?”
